Simple Utah Travel Tips

Check Road Conditions

Review road and construction conditions before leaving, especially during winter or holiday weekends.

Carry Water

Bring enough water for everyone in the vehicle. Southern and eastern Utah can become very hot and dry.

Prepare for Change

Utah weather and temperatures can change quickly. Pack layers, sun protection, and basic emergency supplies.
